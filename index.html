Hagia Sophia

A masterpiece of Byzantine architecture, Hagia Sophia has stood as a cathedral, mosque, and museum. It remains one of the most important landmarks in Istanbul, symbolizing cultural and religious unity across centuries.

The Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome is one of the greatest works of Roman architecture and engineering. This iconic amphitheater hosted gladiator fights and public spectacles, and continues to be a symbol of the Roman Empire’s might and legacy.

Chichén Itzá

Chichén Itzá is an ancient Mayan city located in Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula. Known for the Pyramid of Kukulkán, it was once a major political and economic center, and is now one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
