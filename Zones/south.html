Christ the Redeemer

Brazil

Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer is a colossal statue of Jesus Christ located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, atop the Corcovado Mountain. It stands 98 feet (30 meters) tall, with arms stretching 92 feet (28 meters) wide. Constructed between 1931 and 1936, the statue is made of reinforced concrete and soapstone. It was designed by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, with the sculptor being Paul Landowski..

Date of construction : Between 1931 and 1936.

Machu Picchu

Peru

Machu Pichu

Machu Picchu, an ancient Incan city located in the Andes Mountains of Peru, is known for its stunning terraces, temples, and palaces. Believed to have been built in the 15th century during the reign of Emperor Pachacuti, it remained hidden from the outside world until its rediscovery in 1911 by explorer Hiram Bingham. Today, it is one of the most iconic archaeological sites in the world.

Date of construction: Built in the 15th century.

Christ of the Andes

Argentina/Chile

Christ of the Andes

he Christ of the Andes is a statue of Jesus Christ located on the border between Argentina and Chile in the Andes Mountains. It was inaugurated on March 13, 1904, as a symbol of peace and friendship between the two countries after a border dispute was resolved. The statue stands at 13 feet (4 meters) tall and is made of bronze. The inscription on the statue reads: "Sooner shall these mountains crumble into dust than Argentines and Chileans break the peace sworn at the feet of Christ the Redeemer.".

Date of construction : Inaugurated on March 13, 1904

Chichen Itza

Mexico

Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is an ancient Mayan city located on the Yucatán Peninsula in Mexico. It was one of the most important cities in the Mayan civilization and flourished between the 7th and 10th centuries. The site is best known for the El Castillo pyramid (also called the Temple of Kukulcán), a massive step pyramid dedicated to the feathered serpent god Kukulcán..

Date of construction : 600 AD

The Dome of the Rock

Jerusalem

The Dome of the Rock

The Dome of the Rock, located in Jerusalem, was completed in 691 AD. It is one of the most important and oldest examples of Islamic architecture, built on the Temple Mount. The shrine is revered by Muslims as the site from which the Prophet Muhammad ascended to heaven during the Night Journey. Its stunning golden dome and intricate mosaics make it a significant religious and cultural symbol..

Date of construction : 691 AD

Sagrada Familia

Spain

Sagrada Familia

The Sagrada Familia is a famous basilica located in Barcelona, Spain, designed by the renowned architect Antoni Gaudí. Construction began in 1882, and the church remains unfinished to this day. Gaudí devoted the latter part of his life to the project, incorporating unique architectural and artistic elements inspired by nature and religious symbolism. The Sagrada Familia is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site and is expected to be completed in the 21st century, continuing Gaudí's vision of combining Gothic and Art Nouveau styles into a singular, monumental design..

Date of construction : 1882 (ongoing)

Sydney Opera House

Australia

Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is a world-renowned performing arts center located in Sydney, Australia. Designed by Danish architect Jørn Utzon, its construction began in 1957 and was completed in 1973. The iconic design features a series of white, shell-like sails that have become a symbol of modern architecture. The Sydney Opera House is not only a major cultural venue but also one of the most recognizable architectural landmarks in the world..

Date of construction : 1957-1973

Burj Khalifa

UAE

Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is a skyscraper located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It holds the title of the world's tallest building, standing at a height of 828 meters (2,717 feet). The construction of the Burj Khalifa began in 2004 and was completed in 2010. Designed by architect Adrian Smith, the building features a sleek, modern design and serves as a hub for commercial, residential, and hospitality purposes..

Date of construction: 2004-2010
