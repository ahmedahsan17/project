Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is an active stratovolcano located on the Honshu Island of Japan. It is the highest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters (12,389 feet) and is one of the most recognizable and iconic natural landmarks in the world. Mount Fuji is a revered symbol in Japanese culture, often depicted in art, literature, and spiritual practices.

Date of construction: : Last erupted in 1707-1708 (Edo period), but the mountain itself is believed to have formed over a period of around 100,000 years.

China

Temple of Heaven

The Temple of Heaven is a historic religious complex located in Beijing, China. It was used by emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties for annual ceremonies of prayer for a good harvest. The temple is one of the finest examples of Chinese religious architecture and is known for its symbolic design, representing the relationship between Earth and Heaven.

Date of construction: : 1406 – 1420
